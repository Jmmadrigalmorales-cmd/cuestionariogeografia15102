Weekend Activities, Art & Technology

1. Vocabulary: Art & Technology

Match the definition with the correct word.

1. A painting of a person is called a...
Correct answer: Portrait
2. A painting of the countryside is called a...
Correct answer: Landscape
3. You can paint pictures with a...
Correct answer: Paintbrush
4. An ______ is a program on a phone or tablet.
Correct answer: App
5. You can ______ an app onto your tablet.
Correct answer: Download
6. When you ______ on an app, it opens.
Correct answer: Click

2. At The Weekend

Look at the activities and choose the correct option.

7. We look at old things and art here.
Correct answer: Visit museums
8. We play these with friends (like Uno).
Correct answer: Play card games
9. We cook sweet food in the kitchen.
Correct answer: Make cakes
10. I use a camera or a phone to do this.
Correct answer: Make videos
11. I sit on the sofa and watch TV (football, tennis...).
Correct answer: Watch sport
12. I use a pencil and paper.
Correct answer: Draw pictures

3. Grammar: Present Simple

Complete the sentences with do, don't, or the correct verb.

13. What _____ you do on Saturdays?
Correct answer: do
14. We ______ go to school at the weekend. (Negative)
Correct answer: don't
15. I ______ card games with my friends. (Affirmative)
Correct answer: play
16. We ______ videos. They're really cool!
Correct answer: make
17. I ______ go to school on Sunday.
Correct answer: don't
18. We ______ visit museums. (Negative)
Correct answer: don't

4. Order the sentences

Choose the correct order for the words.

19. you / do / What / at / weekend / the / ?
Correct answer: What do you do at the weekend?
20. visit / I / museums / .
Correct answer: I visit museums.
21. don't / We / school / go / to / .
Correct answer: We don't go to school.
22. make / We / cakes / .
Correct answer: We make cakes.
23. play / I / games / card / .
Correct answer: I play card games.
24. watch / We / films / .
Correct answer: We watch films.

5. Reading Comprehension

Read about Rita and answer.

"Rita paints a painting of her family. Some children think Rita's painting isn't very good. Grace finds the painting. The painting is in the newspaper, on social media and on TV. In the end, everyone loves Rita's work."
25. What does Rita paint?
Correct answer: Her family
26. Do all children like the painting at first?
Correct answer: No
27. Where is the painting? (Choose the one NOT in the text)
Correct answer: Museum (it is not mentioned)
28. In the end, does everyone love Rita's work?
Correct answer: Yes

6. True or False?

29. We go to school on Sundays.
Correct answer: False
30. A 'portrait' is a picture of the countryside.
Correct answer: False (It's a picture of a person)
